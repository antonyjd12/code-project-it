About JD Cucina

A tradition of excellence since 1995

Welcome to JD Cucina, where every meal is prepared with fresh ingredients and served with care. Located in the heart of Kigali, Rwanda, we bring together the rich culinary traditions of Rwanda and Italy.

Our goal is to provide delicious food, excellent service, and a memorable dining experience for every customer. We use only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients from Rwandan farms and traditional family recipes passed down through generations.
